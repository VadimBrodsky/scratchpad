<p># Adding a Unit to a Variable Value</p>
<p>```sass<br />
// A variable with no unit<br />
$variable-with-no-unit: 0;</p>
<p>// Add the unit to the variable, can be used with px, em or %<br />
$variable-with-unit-added: ($variable-with-no-unit * 1%);<br />
```</p>
