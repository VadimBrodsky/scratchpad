<p># Stripping the Unit from a Value</p>
<p>```sass<br />
// A variable with a unit<br />
$variable-with-unit: 0%;</p>
<p>// Strip the unit from the variable<br />
$variable-without-unit: ($variable-with-unit * 1 + 0);<br />
```</p>
